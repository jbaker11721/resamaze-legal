Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my resume as a PDF?

Go to the Create tab, build your canvas, then tap Preview. From the Preview screen, tap the share icon in the top right to export your resume as a PDF.

How do I save a resume version?

In the Create tab, tap the bookmark icon in the top bar of the canvas or preview screen. Give your version a name and tap Save.

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Go to Settings (gear icon) → scroll to Subscription → tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used to purchase.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

The Job Scanner isn't working — what should I check?

Make sure the job description you paste is at least a few sentences long. The scanner needs enough context to make recommendations. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above.
